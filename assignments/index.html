See the full Hybrid exercise list

# Quizzes

Worth 20% of Final Grade

At the start of nearly every class, your instructor will be calling on you to a ask a few questions about the videos and readings assigned the previous week. You should come prepared with at least two questions that will help clarify the topics that you reviewed. Students need to raise their hand to ask your question. The instructor will ask the first person with their hand raised and then ask another student to try to answer.

The objective is to start a discussion and help each other to better understand the referenced material.

Each helpful question is worth 4 of the 20 marks. Over the course of the semester you need to ask 5 questions to get your full 20%.
